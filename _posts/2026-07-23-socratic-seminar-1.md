## Announcements
Please join us for the **very first BitDevs Torino Socratic Seminar**! We are excited to bring the BitDevs format to Turin: an open, technical discussion for students, developers, and researchers interested in the research and development of Bitcoin and related protocols.

Doors open at **19:00** for socializing, and we plan on starting the discussion around **19:10**. The session runs until about 20:00, followed by informal networking (the "Satoshi Spritz").

- **When:** Thursday, 23 July 2026, 19:00–20:00 (CEST)
- **Where:** Turin — venue details on [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/bitdevs-torino/)
- **Language:** English

Follow BitDevs Torino on:

- [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/bitdevs-torino/)
